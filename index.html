How We Work

At Hanachi, you're not just hiring a designer — you're starting a collaborative journey to discover and express your brand's true essence. We begin with a free brand discovery form — not just a checklist, but a thoughtful reflection tool. It helps you clarify your goals, and it helps us understand your business deeply. Next, you’ll meet your dedicated visual identity builder in a comfortable, no-pressure call. This is where we align on your story, your vision, and your brand’s tone. From there, we design with purpose — blending strategy and creativity to craft a bold, authentic identity that’s made to last.
